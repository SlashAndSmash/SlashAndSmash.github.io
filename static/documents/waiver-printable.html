Acknowledgement

By signing this form, I confirm that:

Age: I am aged 18 or over.
Pregnancy: I am not trying to get, or am pregnant.
Cuts: I am aware that I will be handling sharp objects which may result in cuts. I will follow these instructions to minimize my risk:
I will wear gloves when handling broken glass.
I will regularly clean my area of glass fragments by brushing gently or using a wet cloth; I will never blow glass dust or shards off the table.
I will wear safety goggles at all times, as I am aware that breaking glass or using the grinder creates a risk of airborne debris.
Lead: I am aware that lead is a toxic substance, and I will follow these instructions to minimize my risk:
I will not ingest lead or place materials in my mouth.
I will always wash my hands thoroughly after handling lead and before touching my face or food.
I will wear gloves when handling lead.
Burns: I am aware that I will be handling very hot equipment and materials. I will follow these instructions to minimize my risk:
I will hold the soldering iron only by the handle and will never touch the heating element or tip.
I will allow my work to cool after soldering before handling it. If in doubt, I will quench the piece with cool water first.
Fumes: I am aware that soldering releases fumes. I will follow these instructions to minimize my risk:
I will not deliberately breathe in fumes and will keep my face at a safe distance of at least 20cm from the soldering area.
Chemicals: I am aware that chemicals will be used in class. I will follow these instructions to minimize my risk:
I will always wear gloves when handling chemicals.
I will always protect my eyes with goggles.
I will rinse my skin or eyes immediately if I get any chemicals on them.
Physical Fitness: I have declared any relevant medical conditions and have no concerns about my physical ability to participate. If I have concerns I will email slash.and.smash@artlover.com to discuss them before completing this waiver. I am aware that for a full refund, any such issues must be resolved at least 20 days prior to my class.
Assumption of Risk: I understand that while safety equipment and instruction are provided, I participate at my own risk. The instructor is not liable for injuries resulting from a failure to follow safety instructions. If I feel that a task is unsafe, I will stop immediately and speak to my instructor.
Dress Code: I will wear closed-toe shoes, fitted clothing (no baggy sleeves), and tie back long hair.
Data Storage: I consent to my information being stored for up to 12 months for health and safety purposes, as described in the privacy notice.
