## Privacy Notice

**Last updated:** March 2026

### Who I am

I'm Aneurin Warren, running stained glass classes under the name "Slash & Smash." This notice explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information.

### What data I collect

- **Booking information:** your name, email address, and class date (collected via Calendly)
- **Contact form messages:** your name, email address, and message (collected via Formspree)
- **Waiver forms:** your name, signature, and emergency contact details
- **Payment information:** your name and transaction reference (I do not store bank details)

### Why I collect it

- To manage your booking and communicate about your class
- To respond to your enquiries
- To meet health and safety requirements (waiver)
- To process payments

### Who I share it with

- **Calendly** — for booking management ([Calendly Privacy Policy](https://calendly.com/privacy))
- **Formspree** — for contact form processing ([Formspree Privacy Policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/))
- I do not sell or share your data with anyone else

### How long I keep it

- Booking and waiver information: up to 12 months after your class
- Contact form messages: up to 6 months
- I delete your data after these periods unless I have a legal reason to keep it

### Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (right to be forgotten)
- **Object** to how I use your data
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a standard format
